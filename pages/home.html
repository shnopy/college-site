Basic information

We're a fitness centre located just outside Scotland in Kelso town centre, we aim to please with out large range of activities and our multi-purpose halls that can be hired by anyone.

We have been recommended by many papers, such as The Herald and The Telegraph, we host sporting events as can be seen by our calendar. These events are open to all as we have two multi-purpose rooms up for hire which we also use for events.
